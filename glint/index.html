Personal Business Model Canvas

Who helps you?

Who helps me provide value to other are: Friends, colleges, classmates and teachers. My family and these people are supporting me in ways like personal development and technology knowledge.

What you do

Things I do at work are: 3D CAD Design drawing, design constructions and solve problems. I think all of these activities require knowledge about CAD designing and mechanical engineering.

Who you are/ What you have

What I like about my job is that when I solve a problem I get satisfaction to see the working functionality of the application I designed. I'm a person likes working in groups and teams with several people, but I'm also okay with working individually. In my work I like to see a combination of theory and practice. Abilities I do without effort are calculations of applications, I like to solve problems design solutions. I have accounts on several social media like Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram and twitter.

How you help

Values I can give to customers is give them advice about mechanical structures. My strength competences are analyzing, researching and professionalizing. What satisfies me when solving a problem is seeing the functionality of the application I solved. Benefits customers can enjoy when they're working with me. I'm making sure I give right solution. I handle customers with care and continue till the customer and I are satisfied.

How you interact

When I'm working with customers I will make sure we have often contact to ensure they get what they want and don't do unnecessary things. I like to have face to face contact, but I can also work with email and calling. Relationships I have now are most like friends and family relationships.

How they know you/ How they deliver

I'm okay with working with principles of the customers, but I also like to work with my own norms and values. Channels I use most of the time are email and phone calls

Who you help

I'm helping people out who don't have the knowledge to solve the problems they have. I'm helpful and I will use my knowledge to give the customer a result they are content with.

What you give

I'm willing to spend the time till the customer is pleased. When overworking is needed is I will be able to spend more time on a project. Depending on how important the job is I'm willing to give up some personal or family time. My preference goes to regular working times like 7:30-17:00, but here is also some flexibility in. My customers pay for a quality job. They receive a result they satisfied and happy with.
